What is Website Accessibility?

Web Accessibility can be split down into 4 principles which are Perceivable, Operable, Understandable and Robust.

These 4 principles are there in order to show the specfic ascpects of website interfaces that can be improved in order to make websites more accessible and usable.

There are codes made by organisations such as W3 which are a standard of rules on website accessibility and how they must be achieved.
